    <h1>Welcome to the Mack Lab</h1>
    The Mack Lab is interested in how we <strong>learn</strong>. We study how <strong>attention</strong> (selecting relevant information from our experiences) interacts with <strong>memory</strong> (what we know and remember) during learning. Making sense of this interaction informs not only how learning succeeds, but also why it goes awry and how to put it back on track.
